Introduction to HTML5

Welcome to the exciting realm of HTML5, the cornerstone of web development! HTML5, short for Hypertext Markup Language version 5, is integral to structuring and presenting content on the web. It enables us to create everything from straightforward text pages to intricate multimedia experiences. As we explore HTML5 together, we'll simplify its complexities, empowering you to craft your own engaging and interactive webpages. Let's dive into this web development adventure!
